Berkeley Innovation

My work as product developer in this human-centered design club

I applied and got accepted into Berkeley Innovation in September 2014. Using Design Thinking, I will develop a product on a semester-long project for Solano Labs.

Solano Labs is a SaaS with 250+ customers, including airbnb, Stripe, Lumosity, and Change.org. Solano Labs has a 100% retention rate, but could use some help with their onboarding process.

I will be working with Solano Labs to make their onboarding flow simple. This includes user experience research, user interface design, and frontend development to create a prototype.

Check out Berkeley Innovation's website to hear more about this club.
